Delicious Lasagne

Try this easy delicious and quick recipe.

Follow the instructions for a delicious and cheesy lasagne.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Black Olives
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ground Turkey
  • Tomato Paste

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta
  2. Cook Tomato Paste
  3. Grate Cheese
  4. Cut black olives
  5. Preheat Oven to 350 farenheit
  6. Place pasta in oven for 30 minutes.
